Step 1: Download Your Instagram Data

  1. Go to Settings on Instagram.
  2. Tap on Account Center.
  3. Tap on Your Information.
  4. Tap on Download Your Info.
  5. Tap on Download or Transfer Information.
  6. Choose the Instagram account (if you have multiple accounts).
  7. Tap on Some Information.
  8. Scroll to the Connections tab and tap on Followers and Following.
  9. Tap on Download to Device.
  10. Change the settings:
    • Date Range: Last Week
    • Format: JSON
    • Quality: Medium
  11. Tap Create Files.
  12. Wait for Instagram to process your request (you’ll get a notification when it’s ready).
  13. Download the ZIP file without extracting it.
